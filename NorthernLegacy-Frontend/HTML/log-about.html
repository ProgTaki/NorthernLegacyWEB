Game Overview

Northern Legacy is a real-time strategy game inspired by Norse mythology and Viking culture. The game focuses on the management of a Viking village, aiming for continuous development, base building, efficient resource management and strategic warfare against other settlements. The player takes on the role of a Viking leader, making decisions about the life of the village and developing a long-term strategy to ensure the survival and prosperity of his people.
